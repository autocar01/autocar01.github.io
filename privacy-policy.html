Car Rental Platform - Privacy Policy

At Car Rental Platform, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, contact details, driver's license information, payment information, and vehicle details (for car owners). We also gather usage data including rental history, location data, and interactions with our platform.

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us process bookings, verify identities, facilitate secure payments, improve our services, and communicate important updates. We may also use anonymized data for market analysis and business development purposes.

Data Security & Sharing

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. Your information may be shared with insurance providers, payment processors, and rental partners as necessary to fulfill services. We do not sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also opt out of certain data collection practices and marketing communications. Contact our privacy team at goodstobkal@gmail.com to exercise these rights.
